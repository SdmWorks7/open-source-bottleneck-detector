Features

What does
Bottleneck Detector provide?

Unlike generic AI advice, we use real data signals to identify what's actually blocking your progress.

GitHub Evidence
We analyze your real GitHub activity — commits, PRs, issues — to build objective evidence about your actual skill level.
Cognee Memory
Powered by Cognee's knowledge graph — we understand what you tell us semantically, not just keyword matching.
Ranked Bottlenecks
Evidence is scored and ranked by severity. The LLM only explains — it never decides what your bottlenecks are.
